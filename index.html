What's this web app ?

This little web application helps you to find the next manga that you may like the kind and therefore the next manga you will read.

How it's work ?

It's easy ! First, what you have to do it's to type the name of a manga that you like the kind in the search bar above. Next, a maximum of 10 mangas will be recommended to you. These last mangas are suggested according to their kind in common with the searched manga and also to their popularity.
